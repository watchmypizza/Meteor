Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

1. Data Collection Meteor does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The AI features (Ollama) process prompts locally on the host machine. No user messages, server data, or Discord identifiers are stored or transmitted externally by default. 2. Configuration Data Some server-side configurations (e.g., moderation settings, ticket categories, or leveling data) may be stored in Firebase or other databases only if the server administrator sets this up. 3. Use of AI Meteor may use local AI models (e.g., Ollama) for moderation or automod tasks. These models run entirely locally and do not send data to external APIs. AI outputs are generated in real-time and are not logged or analyzed. 4. Third-Party Services If your server connects Meteor to third-party services (e.g., Firebase), their respective privacy policies apply. The Developer is not responsible for those services. 5. Data Security Meteor is open source, and anyone running the Bot is responsible for their own instance security. The official public instance does not log message content or personal information. 6. Children's Privacy Meteor is not directed toward children under 13. Users must comply with Discord's minimum age requirements. 7. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the Bot after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
