Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Meteor handles data when used on Discord servers.

1) Summary

Meteor does not collect, sell, or share personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling. It stores only the minimum configuration needed for features you enable (e.g., moderation settings, level data).

2) What Data May Be Processed

  • Configuration: moderation settings, ticket channels, level/XP data, feature toggles.
  • Identifiers: server, channel, role, and user IDs strictly for bot functionality.
  • Optional logs: moderation or leveling logs when you explicitly enable them.

No message content is analyzed or stored beyond what is necessary to execute commands or automod actions you trigger.

3) Limited Developer Access

The Developer may access stored configuration or leveling data only in limited cases for:

  • Troubleshooting and fixing broken data or restoring data on request,
  • Investigating abuse or violations of these Terms or Discord rules,
  • Maintenance and data migrations.

Any such access is minimal, for operational purposes only, and never for monetization or profiling.

4) AI Usage (Local Only)

Meteor’s AI features use local inference (e.g., Ollama). Data used for AI moderation is processed on the host where the bot runs and is not sent to third-party AI APIs or cloud services. AI outputs are generated in real time and are not retained beyond what is necessary to perform the action.

5) Third-Party Services

If you connect Meteor to a third party (e.g., Firebase), those services process data under their own policies and terms. Configure them responsibly and review their documentation.

6) Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect stored configuration data. If you self-host, you are responsible for securing your instance, secrets, and infrastructure.

7) Retention & Deletion

  • Data is retained only as long as needed for active features.
  • Server owners can request deletion. Removing the bot and contacting the Developer will trigger deletion of stored configuration associated with that server.

8) Children’s Privacy

Meteor is not directed to children under 13 and must be used consistent with Discord’s age requirements.

9) Changes

This Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

10) Contact

Questions or data requests: open an issue on GitHub Issues.
